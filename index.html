How can we help you?

At Secure8, we provide expert-driven services across VAPT, cloud, IAM, and user awareness — helping you stay secure, compliant, and resilient.

Information Security

We protect your digital assets through strategic infosec programs, risk assessments, and compliance-driven security controls.

Penetration Testing

Find weaknesses before hackers do
simulate real-world cyberattacks on your web and mobile applications to uncover vulnerabilities—so you can fix them before they’re exploited.

API Security Testing​​

Secure the connections that power your app
Your APIs are the backbone of your product. We test them for hidden flaws like IDOR, broken authentication, and injection risks to protect your data.

Cloud Security

Protect your AWS, Azure, or GCP environments. We identify misconfigurations and access issues to keep you secure and compliant.

Identity & Access Management (IAM)

Control who has access to what
We help design and secure IAM systems so only the right people access the right resources—reducing insider risk and boosting compliance.

Security Awareness & Managed Services

Build a security-first culture
From team training to 24/7 managed security support, we help you strengthen internal defenses and meet industry standards like ISO 27001 and GDPR.

Mobile Application Security

Protect your Android and iOS apps from cyber threats
We uncover vulnerabilities, insecure data handling, and broken authentication flows to ensure your mobile applications remain secure and trusted by users.
